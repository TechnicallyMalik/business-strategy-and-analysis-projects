# California Data Center Water Regulation Analysis

## Overview
This project documents a business and public policy analysis focused on whether California should regulate water use by massive data centers. The work evaluates the issue through business, government, and societal perspectives, with attention to environmental sustainability, transparency, stakeholder impact, and policy response.

## Course Context
- MGMT 370
- Group Project

## What the Project Examined
- The growth of large data centers in California
- Water consumption tied to cooling and energy use
- The policy debate around disclosure and regulation
- Assembly Bill 93 and its proposed reporting requirements
- Stakeholder impacts across business, government, and society
