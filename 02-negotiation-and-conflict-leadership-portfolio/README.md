# Strategic Negotiation and Conflict Leadership Portfolio

## Overview
This project documents a cross-cultural negotiation strategy brief developed for terrazzo procurement in a commercial construction context. The work focuses on negotiation design, approval authority, sourcing risk, delivery timing, communication strategy, ethics, and operational alignment.

## Course Context
- MGMT 320
- Negotiation and Conflict Management

## What the Project Examined
- Cross-cultural negotiation planning
- Procurement risk and delivery timing
- Decision authority and approval structure
- Communication strategy across parties
- Ethical and reputational safeguards
- Value creation through structured trade design

## What I Did
- Built a cultural comparison snapshot to anticipate negotiation differences
- Developed a phase-by-phase negotiation approach
- Designed value-creating trade options instead of focusing only on price
- Created communication, escalation, and recovery protocols
- Added ethics checks and written follow-up structure

## Skills Demonstrated
- Negotiation strategy
- Cross-cultural communication
- Risk analysis
- Stakeholder and power awareness
- Executive writing
- Ethical decision-making
- Operational planning

## Files Included
- Negotiation strategy brief PDF

## Key Takeaway
This project strengthened my ability to design a structured negotiation strategy that balances cultural awareness, operational execution, written clarity, and leadership-level decision support.
